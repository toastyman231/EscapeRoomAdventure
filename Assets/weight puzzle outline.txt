4 weights, 1 locked behind puzzle

Weights - 1, 5, 7, 10

Must find weight of the 7-weight to get code for door

Logic puzzle where 4 people own the weights, must figure out which weights in the game
correspond with the weights in the puzzle by weighing them

Once you figure out which weight is which, solving the puzzle will tell you the exact weight
of the 7-weight, giving you the solution.
